## Source Preference

Best source types:

- Court records
- Government reports
- Official transcripts
- Primary video clips
- Reputable investigative journalism
- Nonprofit civil rights or environmental organizations
- Public data dashboards

Avoid using:

- Unsourced social media screenshots
- AI-generated claims
- Out-of-context clips
- Articles that only quote another article without linking the original
